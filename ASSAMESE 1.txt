ASSAMESE 1 Bigyan hol manuhor basebati xabhyatar atyai guruttopurn aspaṇḍa. Aji bigyanor bikashor phalei, amra aadhunik jiwanot anek baisistya aru suvidhare anubhav kori asu. Bijuli, mobile phone, internet, computer ar robotics — ei hokolutei bigyanor obadan. Tarkot, anarokom jukti-buĵhi manuh aji poth otiloh concerning upagrah abiskar kori, eihii prokriyate manuhor jibone notun notun subhidha aneche.

Prachin manuh poth boja chesta kori, kintu bigyan nasile sehi chinta-kothar sothik uttor pete paribo ne. Jesey bristi kene hoy, bijuli kene utpanna hoy — ei dharanaguli proman-base soch kore bigyan. Ei proman-base bigyan aji se sob jinish manuhor kase bistarito bikhit kore dise.

Chikitsha khetre bigyanor abodan obimaar. Munpur komor dardhe manuh mrito howa hob, kintu aji doctor diagnosis, operation ar organ transplantation moto notun podhati bebohar kori, anek manuhor jibon rokkha korte pare. Cancer, HIV, COVID–19 — ei roger bidesh prithibir bajhiyete bigyan chikitsha poth khule dise.

Communication khetre mobile ar internet manuhor jibone suhali subhidha niyeche. Samay onek dikhe take transaction text ar video call kore bishwo manuher shathe jogajog korte paribo. Prothom kali manuh chithi lekhe, tak edge neyar babe din ak din lage. Kintu aji ek click e international call ar email patha jai.
