Manogat swasthya ha aajcha samayacha atyavashyak mudda aahe. Aapan jashā shaareerik swasthyaache kaleje ghetoy, tasach mansik aaram va anand yanche mahattva hi samajun ghene gharjeche aahe. Stress, anxiety, depression, burnout he phokine fakt tondavar aajun takna nahi—parantu hi samasya aaj pratyeka vekti kannyet, manaat va hrudayat pahate aahe.

Aajcha digital yug maage lifestyle chya badalta patterns mule jivan adik fast ani competitive zhale aahe. Na okha job competition, na social media pressure, na relationship stress—ahe sagle lokanchya zevanavar bhar yethe. Yala manage negate karane javalpas avashyak ho jate. Vyayama, yogasana, meditation, prakritiche mel—iske saathi manogat swasthya maintain karne madat karte. Samajik khupe bharav, kutumbasachya sampark, manogat vartan… haa sagla stress levels kami karate.

Mansik aarogya jar nantrmodha asel tar vardi karanarya sanket jase, nidra nasnal, sthirata ghatne, mood swings, apathy, concentration problem—hya lakshan aaplyala kalat yetat. Tule vilambit nivaran nahi kelatar yanchya phale lokancha day-to-day life, relationships, work productivity mittatil. Ya mukhyan lakshanna olkhat, avashyak tet mahiti round-the-clock counselling services, doctors, psychiatrists va psychologists peksha madat ghene jaruri aahe.

Ayurveda, yoga va pranayama jase technique madhe ek regular schedule madhe practice kela tar ek holistic approach milte—physical, mental va emotional level var negative thoughts reduce honto. BA psychology tukadacha therapy—CBT (cognitive behavioral therapy), mindfulness-based stress reduction, group therapy !! ya sarvancha yoga madhe kucit shikshan milel.
