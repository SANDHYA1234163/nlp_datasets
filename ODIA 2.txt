ODIA 2 Odia sanskruti re aneka utsaba o parba achhi, kintu mu sabuthu besi Raja parba ku bhalapaye. Raja parba hochi Odia lokara eka ananya parba jaha prakruti, stri-shakti o ananda sahita jorita.

Raja tinta dina chali thaye — Pahili Raja, Raja Sankranti, o Basi Raja. Ehi parba re Odia ghare ananda, utsaha o saundrya chhayi thae. Kanyamananku ei dina bohu bhabare sammana dia jaye. Prakrutire ei samaya re nabina rupe prakatita haanti, atei ei samaya re bhumi chasha pain aram paba. Stri ku ei samayare prithibi ro mata hisabe dekha jaye.

Ehi parbare bisista khadya madhya bahut prachur. Pitha-pana mane — poda pitha, chakuli pitha, arisa pitha, manda pitha ghare banuchi. Kanyamananku nua bastra dia jaye, se mane dolire buhli-buhli khela khelanti, gana ganti, raja geeta gaanti.

Raja re tala ghara safai, choka arakha, decoration sabu madhya dekha jaye. Lokamani chhota o bada samaste ranga rangare bhagi neanti. Raja geeta madhya ghuñcha o tala tale gaijaanti. Ei samaya re kheti karma o anyanya kathina karma badha jaye, eha hochi visranti o ananda ra samaya.

School o college madhya chhuti heijaye, tahele chhatra o chhatri mane ghare basa ananda upabhoga karanti. Mu madhya mora paribara sahita ei dinati ghare bahut ananda re katiye. Mu mora maa sahita pitha banaye, bhagini sahita dolire bosi Raja geeta gaye.

Raja parba samajare eka samata ra sandesha dei thae. Ehi dina pratyeka stri o kanya ku bhinna rupe dekha jaye, tanka shraddha o maryada dia jaye. Eti prakruti o stri ra milita utsaba. Eti ru lokamananku prem, daya, samanta o pratibha ra patha sikhaye.
