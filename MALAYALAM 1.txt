MALAYALAM 1:
Bigyaanam manushyanude jeevithathil oru mahatthaya maattam konduvannittundu. Aadhunika bigyaanam manushyanude jeevithathinte ella thalangalilum vechitulla prabhaavam vismayakaram aanu. Innu nammal anubhavikkunna sughamulla jeevithathinte pinnale undakunnathu bigyaanathinte valiya thangal aanu. Udaharanamayi, chikitsa bigyaanam, janajeevanam, sancharavum, shiksha, krishi ennivayil bigyaanathinte valarchcha nammalude jeevithathilekku sugham konduvannittundu.

Pracheena kaalamile manushyar aakaasham nokki chinthikkunnu: suryan enthina ezhunellunnu? mazhayenthina peyyunnu? theeyude utpatti engane? Ee chodyangalkkulla uttarangal labhikkunnathinu avarkku mattullavarude vakkukalil viswasikkendiyirunnu. Paksha bigyaanam ee ellam thettaya dharanakalaya maatti. Bigyaanam ennu parayunnathu prakrithiyude gyaanam aanu. Ee gyaanam anubhavavum pariikshayum yuktiyum upayogichu netiyathanu. Galileo, Newton, Einstein pole bigyaanikal puthiyaputhiya sidhaanthangal vazhi bigyaanathinu puthiyoru disha koduthirunnu. A sidhaanthangal manushyanude jeevitham sughapradamayi maatti.
