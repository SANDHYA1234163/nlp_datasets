skulot mor egoraki bandhawi aasil. nam tair bulbul.protito shreneete dui tinibar fel karihe opar shreneeloi uthisil. seibabei taik ami felari buliyei matisilo. seibuli kole tai lajo napai, beyao napai. seidinar katha. amar buronjeer porikhaa aasil. eai shrenitot bulbolor tinibosor hol.eibar pas nokorile skulor pora kheda khab lagib. moi eai shrenitote bulbulok lag paisohi.amar ghoror osarare jetiya amar agore porai bondhutto. ami dujoni porikhaa diboloi goi thakote moi buronjeer chon tarikh bilak aaurai goi aaso tair kintu khoborei nai. aapon mone goi aase. enete ami goi thakote dekhilo je ejon bura manuh rastar majot pori aase.osare paajore kono nai. ami douri goi dekhilo manuh jon cyclor pora pori moorfati tej olaise. etiyai hospitalloi nib lagib. ifale porikhaaloio besi deri nai.
