BENGALI 2: 
Gramer jibon holo ekṭi sadharon ebong shanto jibon. Ei jibone prakritir nikatata ache, manusher modhye mohimā ache. Gram holo prachin samajer sthāponar ekṭi udaharan, jekhane manush ekjon arekjonke chene, sahajoge bāche. Diner shuru hoy prakritir madhur dhwani diye — kukur jāgar, pakhi chahāni, gaṛur gābhīr dhoni.

Gram er manush sara din porishrom kore. Sakale khete jāy, khete giye māṭi chāṣ kore. Jomi chāṣ hobar madhyame tāra nijeder khabar utpadon kore. Krishi holo gram er pradhan jibika. Dhan, gandhamula, sabji, phol — sob kichu tāra nijeder haatei toiri kore. Goru, chhagol, murgi palan o ekṭi guruttopurno angsha.

Gramer shishu ra prakritir kole boro hoy. Tara khelar madhyame sikhte pare, sṛjanśīl hoy. Gram e thaka chhele-meye ra ekṭi anandamoy bātoyāne bāḍe. Skul khub ekṭi sahaj o nimno stharer hoy, kintu sikhār icchā prachur thake. Mastermashai ra śraddhay bhora.

Gramer nārīra gharer kāj, krishikāj o hāt bazarer byabosthāya jog den. Tārā māṭi chāṣ, sabji toiri, chulano, ranna, jal ānār moto porishram sange kare. Tārā nimno kharche cholā chesta kare, tabuo sushil chārître.

Gramer manush onek sohoj. Tārā apod bipod e ekjon arekjoner sahajyokari hoy. Gram e kono prarthonā sabai mile kare. Puja-parban sabai mile palan kore. Ekhane moner modhye kono phāṭ nei, kono jogajog er obhab nei.

Grame pratiti ritu alāda bhabe prokāsh pai. Borshā ritu te dhaner khete pāni, nāo bhāsā, srabon dhārā. Hemanto te shitey katha, sishirer prabhāb. Bosonto e phuler gandho, nobo pallober joygān. Gramer māṭi, hāowa, jal — sab kichu mṛdu, mānuṣik ebong prakritik.

Tobu, gramer kichu samasya ache. Gramer rasta khub bhalo noy, chikitsha byabostha kom, bidyut o pani samasya ache. Bāzār durotto beshi thakā e anek samoy osubidha hoy. Skul o chikitsha kendro nei prāy gramer kachākachhi.
