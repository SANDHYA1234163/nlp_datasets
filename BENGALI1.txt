Biggyaan holo manusher jiboner ekṭi pradhan shakti. Aaj amra je suvidha‑jukt jibon jachhi, tar pichhone biggyaanik unnayoner onek prabhab chhupi ache. Bijuli, computer, internet, mobile, robot‑technology—ei sob e biggyaan er obadan. Puratana jughe manush brishti keno hoy, bijli keno utpanna hoy ei dharona bojhte pareni; kintu biggyaan ei gokhane alok feleche.

Chikitsha khetre biggyaan er unnati manusher ayu barie diyeche. Daktar‑doctor operation, organ transplant, robotic surgery—ei sob aaj real. Cancer, heart attack, diabetes rogeo taroter unoishchit bhabe chikitsha samadhan toiri hoyeche. Vaccine, antiviral, antibiotic development er phale janamontri rogbidroho kona upaye chole eseche.

Jogajoger khetre mobile o internet manusher dhoren na seirokom poriborton eneche—ejono ekti click e manush prithibir bipulp kache jukto. Email theke social media, video call—biggyaan er ei sab uplobdhi amader jibon ke khub nirbam kore diyeche.

Shikkha khetreo biggyaan darun ek unioche. Online learning, virtual classroom, digital library gulo bina ekti grade, thi ekti systeme bound hoye jawar sujog direche. Google, Khan Academy, YouTube—ei sob platform theke amra kichu sikhechi. College, university hote ber hoina — smartphone theke purono physics, math, language—sob kisu sekha jay.

Krishi khetre biggyaan er upadan khub guroto. Hybrid seed, chemical fertilizer, drip irrigation, mechanisation er phale manusher fasol osshoyge barano jay. Drone theke remote‑sensing, satellite‑monitoring er sahajye fasol monitorkora jay. Ei sab prakriya chashi ke samriddhi niyeche.

Transportation o boltei platform er upore chole. Plane, train, metro, bullet train—sob kotha biggyaan er plastic ar purano upadan er upor nirbhar koreche. Space research—Chandrayaan‑2, Mission‑Mars er jonne biggyaan er simulation ar deep learning aaj dristanto.
