ASSAMESE 2
Siksha hol manuhor personal ar samuhik unnitir mooladaṇḍa. Jodi shikkha nashe, tar phale manuhor jibon komja ar social responsibility kumja hoy. Siksha mukh gun, kritimottar thinking, emotional intelligence, moral values ar social responsibility create kore. Ei hokolutei samajik instead healthier dynamic nurture pay.

Aj apuni dekhte paribo je education sector aji digital transformation experience koruche. Online platform – Zoom, Google Classroom, Microsoft Teams – ei base education seamlessly ongoing ase, khub subhodoy ar inclusive. Ei procure paribesh rural-urban gap hariye dite sahajjo kore, jekhunu student internet ar smartphone thakile education engage korte pare.

Curriculum theke beyond cognitive learning, emotional social learning [SEL], critical thinking, problem-solving, collaborative projects — ei jinish ar upor emphasis thakibo lage. Karne aji jibon complex hoy, student ke probability analysis, data interpretation, environmental stewardship ar social justice free coverage diya hoibo. Ei comprehensive approach anke bacho ke adaptable ar future-ready manuh tento grow koribo pare.

Teachers ar instructors ehi pathotchange adaptation koribo lage. Traditional chalk-board system evolve kari, interactive whiteboard, VR/AR, simulation-based learning — ei subhidha facilitate kore. Jodi teacher subject matter knowledge ba pedagogical digital skills na thake, tahole efficiency drop hoy. Tai teacher development programs ar regular training essential — e.g., integrating learning management system (LMS), online assessment tools, analytics.

Higher education ar research sector aji collaboration-mode. University, industry ar government partnership facilitate R&D, incubation center, entrepreneurship, applied research. Student research telehealth, agri-tech, renewable energy, data science — ei dubi vrata theke innovative solutions come kore ase. Ei link university-ar manpower, local economy ar developed society major support kore.
