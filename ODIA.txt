janmara chari dasandhi aji ateeta sata, hele bismruta nuhen ajayara manasapataru. nija matrugarbharu nisruta hoi prathama thara pain dharanni maa'ra kollare jeunthi pada thapithila se dine,sei ta'ra gan kramabikasara sopana dein aji sabhyatara pragatisila rajaraastaare chalachanchala. bibartanasila duniare sthoola paribartana aneka, hele sukhma chetanara paribartanara dharaa besh khinna pratitahue amara ehi manabiya simeeta ayusa paridhire.paribartita ruchi, paripati, jibanasaili, abhimukhya o samajikatare bikasita sabhyata purunna dadaraa kaanthare nuaaa chuna lagila pari prachina sanskaara, sanskruti upare eka nua rangeen khollapaa sama manehue,hele sehi nutanakhollapaa bhitare prachinatara basna ebebi ajayara manepade o eka romanchita anuvuti re manaprana pulakita hoiutthe ta'ra.
