MALAYALAM 2:
Prakriti oru divya varadanam aanu. Manushyanude jeevitham prakritiyil ninnanu thudangunnathu. Vanangal, puzhakal, parvathangal, marangal, pakshikal, mrugangal, ithokke prakritiyude angangal aanu. Ee bhoomi manushyanum mattullavarkkum jeevikkunna sthalamaanenkil athinu karanam prakritiyude samriddhiyum saukhyavum aanu.

Innu, valare valiya oru samasya aanu prakriti nashtappedunnathu. Vanamaram murikkalum, pradushanavum, plastic upayogavum ithine kaaranam aayi. Manushyan avashyamillatha vazhikalil prakritiyude niyantranam nashtappetuttunnu. Ee reethiyil pokunna padhathi manushyan thanne nashtappeduthum.

Prakritiyude samrakshanam nammude kartavyam aanu. Maram ittalum, plastic upayogam kurakkukayum, kudimazha vellam samrakshikkukayum cheyyanam. Janangal orumichu prakritiyude kshemaartham chinthichal mathram ee bhoomi valare nannayi nilanirthan kazhiyum.

Kuttikalil ninnum mozhangal vare ellaavarum prakritiyude moolyam manasilakkanam. Schoolukalilum samuhathilum prakriti samrakshanathinu viseshadhyayanangal nadathendathu undu. Ee bhoomi nammade aanu, athinal athine rakshikkuka ennathanu sreshtamaya dharmam.
